Chestnut Run Farm

Chestnut Run Farm is a winery in Pilesgrove Township in Salem County, New Jersey that produces Fuji apple and Asian pear wines. The apple and pear trees were first planted in 1986 as part of a specialty produce farm.

Address: 66 Stewart Road, Pilesgrove, NJ 08098

Southwind Vineyard & Winery

Southwind Vineyard and Winery specializes in wines made in the Bordeaux style. We strive to make wines that are deep in color, lush in viscosity and body, with a balance of discreet tannins and crisp acidity. The wines we are most proud of to date are the Bloody Hand Cabernet Sauvignon which is rich and lush without too much oak, Joe's favorite. The second is a Blanc de Noir, meaning a white or Rose wine made from black (red) grapes.
